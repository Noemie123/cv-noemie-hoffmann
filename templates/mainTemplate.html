L'anglais

I acquired a good level of English as I have studied it for years. I always loved English, the language as well as the culture. I passed a degree of English literature and civilisation in 2015. After that, I worked in England for a while. I had an internship in a company in York and was working with the Human Resources. Among other things, I created posters about health for the employees. I also worked at improving the hiring system, listing all the steps to employment to see how long it takes and how to make it more efficient. In October 2015, I started working as a French teacher assistant in Leicester. I worked in three different schools, with students from 11 to 18 years old. I was helping them learning French, preparing them for exams or competitions and I also managed a little group to produce a newspaper in French.

In my free time, I am also lucky enough to go to England quite often. I love the culture, the architecture, the landscape. When I am not travelling, I spend a lot of time using the language, be it by reading or watching videos or films in English. To be honest, I love languages in general. I speak a little bit of Spanish as well though I understand way better than I can speak. And during my studies as well as because of personal interest, I was able to start learning different languages such as Chinese, Dutch or even Polish. I cannot really speak any of those right now but I will try to make some time to learn a new language.

But my forte stays English. I haven't got any language diploma to testify my current level (except from my degree) but I took some positioning test on the internet and it turns out I am either C1 or C2 (European levels) which are the best levels you can have in a language. Thus, working with English doesn't bug me. I can easily find my ways, look for information as well as write or talk.

Loisirs

J'essaie de passer le plus de temps possible avec mes amis et ma famille. Quand ce n'est pas le cas, je dessine ou alors je lis. Je joue aussi, souvent sur mon téléphone, principalement à des jeux de logique. J'aime tout ce qui est sudoku, mots croisés, rubixcube... J'écoute aussi beaucoup de musique. Je continue de pratiquer l'anglais aussi, en regardant des vidéos ou bien en lisant.
